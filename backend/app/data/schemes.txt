ELIGIBILITY: BE/BTech for Engineer Trainee. ITI for Apprentice. First class required. Age limit 27 years for engineers. Age relaxation for reserved categories.
BENEFITS: Engineer Trainee salary Rs 40,050 to Rs 1,40,000 per month. Apprentice stipend as per Apprenticeship Act.
DOCUMENTS: Engineering degree or ITI certificate, age proof, category certificate, Aadhaar.
DEADLINE: Notification on BHEL website.
APPLY AT: https://bhel.com
STATES: Delhi, Telangana, Tamil Nadu, Madhya Pradesh, Jharkhand, Uttarakhand, Kerala, Punjab, Uttar Pradesh

SCHEME: ONGC Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Recruitment
CATEGORY: Government Job / Energy
ELIGIBILITY: BE/BTech or BSc for various posts. Also ITI for apprentice posts. Age limit 30 years. First class required.
BENEFITS: Various technical and non-technical posts. Starting salary Rs 40,000 to Rs 1,80,000 per month depending on grade. Oil sector perks and facilities.
DOCUMENTS: Degree or ITI certificate, age proof, category certificate, Aadhaar.
DEADLINE: Notification on ONGC website.
APPLY AT: https://ongcindia.com
STATES: All India - major centres in Gujarat, Maharashtra, Assam, Andhra Pradesh

SCHEME: PMKVY Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana
CATEGORY: Skill Development / Job
ELIGIBILITY: Indian nationals, school or college dropouts, unemployed youth. Age 15 to 45 years. No income limit. Priority to economically weaker sections.
BENEFITS: Free skill training in 40 plus sectors. Rs 8,000 monetary reward on successful completion and assessment. Job placement assistance and recognition of prior learning.
DOCUMENTS: Aadhaar card, bank account, photograph, educational qualification documents.
DEADLINE: Ongoing enrollment throughout the year
APPLY AT: https://pmkvyofficial.org or nearest PMKVY training center
STATES: All India

SCHEME: National Apprenticeship Training Scheme NATS
CATEGORY: Skill Development / Job
ELIGIBILITY: Diploma or degree holders in engineering, technology, or vocational courses. Age 16 to 35 years. Registered on NATS portal.
BENEFITS: Stipend Rs 9,000 to Rs 14,000 per month during apprenticeship in PSUs and private companies. Practical work experience and certificate from BOAT/BOPT.
DOCUMENTS: Degree or diploma certificate, Aadhaar, bank account, photograph.
DEADLINE: Registration open throughout the year. Companies post apprenticeship vacancies.
APPLY AT: https://nats.education.gov.in
STATES: All India

SCHEME: C-DAC PG Diploma in Advanced Computing PG-DAC
CATEGORY: Skill Development / Job
ELIGIBILITY: BE or BTech or MCA or MSc graduates. Admission through C-CAT entrance exam. Any engineering or computer science background eligible.
BENEFITS: Industry-ready software training for 6 months. Excellent placement record with top IT companies. Course fee subsidized. Average placement package Rs 4 to 8 lakh per year.
DOCUMENTS: Graduation certificate, C-CAT scorecard, Aadhaar, photograph.
DEADLINE: February batch applications in November. September batch applications in June.
APPLY AT: https://cdac.in
STATES: Maharashtra, Karnataka, NCR Delhi, West Bengal, Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Telangana, Madhya Pradesh

SCHEME: Stand Up India Scheme for SC ST and Women Entrepreneurs
CATEGORY: Startup / Job Creation
ELIGIBILITY: SC or ST borrowers and women entrepreneurs above 18 years. First time enterprise only. Must be setting up greenfield enterprise in manufacturing, services, or trading.
BENEFITS: Bank loans between Rs 10 lakh and 1 crore for new businesses. Composite loan covering 75 percent of project cost. Handholding support from SIDBI and NABARD.
DOCUMENTS: SC/ST or gender certificate, business plan, identity proof, address proof, Aadhaar, bank account.
DEADLINE: Ongoing - apply anytime
APPLY AT: https://standupmitra.in or any Scheduled Commercial Bank
STATES: All India
